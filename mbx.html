How does it work?

Simply start with feeding clientele info into your system via APIs; and leave the rest on Multibureau’s X technology. It recognizes information you put in, and regardless of the data’s uniformity; you will get accurate credit scores from all bureaus in a matter of seconds.

What MBx can do for you

AUTOMATIC DATA FORMATTING

The format in which each credit bureau needs its info is different. While the difference in arrangement can be miniscule; it can also be big enough to act as a hindrance to achieve straight through processing. Multibureau X collects the data that you put in only one entry source- your loan origination system. It configures this data to fill in the requirements of all the credit bureaus; automatically. Why spend hours to format different feeds for each bureau when you can get speedy one-stop results?

INBUILT RULE ENGINE

On your dashboard; you can choose the bureau to which requests are to be sent as per your credit policy.
For example: if you want to run an enquiry with three bureaus only if the loan surpasses 7 lakhs and just two bureaus if lower that amount; you set it up right on your dashboard. If you want to run CIBIL reports for metro city applicants, but Equifax for other clients; you can adjust it accordingly. All this, with just a few rule changes with no dependence on the IT team.

ANALYSING DATA

MultiBureau X studies the information on your system and gives you analytical feedback that you can work with. If one credit bureau only generates credit scores for just 50% of your Mumbai clients, it will recognize the pattern. Likewise, it will show you stats that you can then analyse further.

What’s more, this data can be downloaded off the frontend with no intervention of our staff.
